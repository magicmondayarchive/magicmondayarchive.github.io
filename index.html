Search tips

By default, all words must appear somewhere in the comment, but can be in any order.

Exact phrase — words must appear in the order you typed them. e.g. useful to match "middle pillar" as one term.

Whole word — words won't match on substrings. e.g. "MP" won't match on "impressive".

Case sensitive — "Magic" won't match "magic".

Regex — advanced search using regex. e.g. "middle pillar|\bmp\b" will match either "middle pillar" or "mp".


Highlighted matches — clicking on a matched comment will update the right-side nav to that match.
